PYTHAGORAS &amp; THE BROTHERHOOD (c. 570–495 BCE) — LOGIKĒ, the FRONT (between [[thales-of-miletus]] and [[aristotle]]). Thales proved the first theorem; the Pythagoreans made PROOF a way of life and staked a creed on it: NUMBER — whole numbers and their ratios — is the substance of reality. Real evidence: musical HARMONY is whole-number ratio (octave 2:1, fifth 3:2, fourth 4:3). But their own devotion to proof produced the discovery that BROKE the creed — a clean REDUCTIO AD ABSURDUM that √2 (the diagonal of a unit square) is NO ratio of whole numbers: the first famous proof by contradiction, the deductive engine of all logic. LIVE: (1) step the √2 irrationality proof (assume p/q in lowest terms → p²=2q² → p even → q even → both even → contradicts lowest terms; rational approximations 1, 3/2, 7/5, 17/12… crowd √2 but their squares never reach 2, verified); (2) the consonant musical ratios on a string (click to hear the interval). CONFIDENCE-TIERED: STRONG (√2's irrationality is a real, early proof-by-contradiction; the consonance ratios are real; the cult of proof shaped Greek math) / THE-MAN-IS-A-FOG (Pythagoras wrote nothing; 'Pythagoras' = the brotherhood; the theorem a²+b²=c² was used by Babylonians ~1000 yrs earlier) / MYSTICISM (number-worship, transmigration, secrecy; ⚠ the irrationality result is credited to HIPPASUS, whom legend says the order drowned for revealing it). ⚑ A community devoted to proof followed its own method to a truth it could not bear — the tool stronger than the creed.
